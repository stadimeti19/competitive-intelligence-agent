# Competitive Intelligence Assistant

Competitive Intelligence Assistant is an agentic market-intelligence platform that automates competitor research, company analysis, and thesis-driven opportunity discovery. It uses multi-agent LLM workflows, web scraping, public-source enrichment, and structured scoring to turn a company, industry, or investment thesis into competitor profiles, feature comparisons, pricing insights, visualizations, saved reports, and ranked company recommendations.

I built this after realizing that manual competitive intelligence research was taking too long. I wanted a faster way to research companies for both investing in my personal portfolio and doing competitor analysis for other projects, without manually searching dozens of sources, comparing features, and synthesizing strategic takeaways each time.

The system includes a React/FastAPI dashboard for generating CI reports and a thesis radar for ranking companies against custom theses. It supports source-backed evidence, confidence signals, configurable research depth, Supabase-backed saved runs, and interactive exploration of competitive landscapes.
